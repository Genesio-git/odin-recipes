Description:

It's a dish that I really like, that's what I have to say.

Ingredients

  • 3 Diced chicken breasts
  • 1 clove of chopped garlic
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 Tablespoons (soup) of mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup mustard
  • 1 Cup mushrooms
  • 1 Cup cream
  • potato straw to taste

Steps:

  1. In a saucepan, mix the chicken, garlic, mayonnaise, salt and pepper.
  2. In a large frying pan, melt the butter and brown the onion.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken until golden.
  4. Add the mushrooms, ketchup and mustard.
  5. Incorporate the cream and remove from the fire before boiling.
  6. Serve with white rice and straw potatoes.
