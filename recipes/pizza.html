Ingredients:

Mass:

  • 250 G of wheat flour
  • 150 Ml of warm water
  • 2 Tablespoons of organic yeast
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil

Sauce:

  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 2 peeled and seedless ripe tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pinch of sugar

Coverage:

  • Italian salami, finely sliced
  • Mozzarella cheese in slices
  • 1/2 bell pepper in slices
  • Calabrian pepper (optional)

Steps:

Mass:

  1. Dilute the yeast, salt and sugar in the warm water as instructed on the yeast package.
  2. Mix with the flour and stir with your hands in a bowl until a consistent dough is formed.
  3. Bake the dough well for about 5 minutes.
  4. Let the dough sit for 40 minutes so it can grow.
  5. With a roll of pasta open the dough in the desired pizza format with the thickness of 0.5 cm.
  6. Pre-serve in the oven at 180¬∞C for 5 minutes.

Sauce:

  1. Beat the tomatoes with garlic and onion.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan and saute the processed ingredients.
  3. After braising, add the water and the pinch of sugar and cook until full bodied in the desired consistency.

Assembly:

  1. Distribute the tomato sauce over the pre-baked dough.
  2. Top with sliced mozzarella cheese and spread over the slices of Italian salami, peppers and onions (optional) in a slice over the cheese.
  3. Sprinkle with pepper.
  4. Drizzle with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and bake in the oven very hot for 12 minutes, or until golden filling.
